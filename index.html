Welcome to Cedar Creek Ski Park

In the hills of Elkins, Arkansas, Cedar Creek Water Ski Park is a premier, family-run facility featuring two man-made three-event tournament lakes. We specialize in slalom, trick, and jump, offering world-class coaching, professional driving, and a welcoming environment for skiers of all levels.

Whether you are a beginner learning to stand up on skis or a seasoned athlete chasing your next personal best, Cedar Creek is the place to grow, train, and enjoy the thrill of water skiing. We proudly host tournaments, offer personalized lessons, and run intensive ski camps throughout the season.

We are also the proud home of the University of Arkansas Waterski Club Team.

Come experience the water like never before - let’s ski!
